What is Pravilo?

Pravilo is a training system where a person is gently suspended horizontally in the air with cuffs on their hands and feet. This setup uses your own body weight and gravity to create a uniform, deep-body decompression. The horizontal stretching helps return spinal structures to their natural position, freeing compressed nerves and blood vessels. Simultaneously, it stretches and normalizes the tone of muscles, fascia, and ligaments, providing profound relief and realignment.

Who is Pravilo for?

Pravilo is beneficial for a wide range of people, from athletes looking to enhance performance and recovery, to office workers seeking relief from a sedentary lifestyle. It is particularly effective for individuals dealing with non-acute back pain, spinal misalignments (like scoliosis), osteochondrosis, and joint stiffness. Essentially, anyone who wants to become stronger, more flexible, and feel better in their body can benefit.
